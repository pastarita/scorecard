## Blueprint Format

Each prompt follows a three-part structure:

1. **Output Frame** — defines layout, panel count, or compositional arrangement.
2. **Semantic Payload** — enumerates the essential cross-domain elements that must appear.
3. **Domain Mapping** — specifies golf, mathematical, and LLM correlations to maintain ontological alignment.
